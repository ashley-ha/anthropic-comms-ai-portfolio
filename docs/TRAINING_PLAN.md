# Team Training Plan

Audience: Communications team members with mixed technical backgrounds.

## Session 1: Fundamentals (45 min)

- What each tool does and does not do.
- How to interpret confidence/priority outputs.
- When to escalate manually.

## Session 2: Workflow Drills (60 min)

- Run digest and review selected clips.
- Practice rapid-response playbooks with sample incidents.

## Reinforcement

- Office hours: weekly for 4 weeks.
- Shared FAQ and known issues list.
- Short adoption survey at day 14 and day 30.
